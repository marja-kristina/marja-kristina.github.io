I enjoy traveling and have visited over 30 countries. You can see where I have been on the map below.

Create your own visited countries map.
I have volunteered for the International Dublin Gay Theatre festival for five years in a number of roles, as a Venue Manager (2015-2019), PR Officer (2015) and Box Office Supervisor (2015-2016). I have learned a lot about customer service and volunteer management during my time with the festival.

I have also volunteered as Entertainment Officer at the Silk Road International Film Festival and Volunteer/Sponsorship Assistant at the St. Patrick's Festival in 2018, and Event & Social Media Volunteer at Five Lamps Arts Festival in 2016.

In the past, I have organized and helped run events for Democrats Abroad Ireland (a fundraising quiz and an election night watch party in 2016), and was the secretary of the Wicklow Branch of Social Democrats from 2018 to 2019, taking minutes at meetings and getting in touch with past and new members.
I have been interested in genealogy for almost 15 years. I have extensively researched my husband's family tree, have made many contacts with his cousins around the globe, and have visited some charming small villages in England on the ancestral hunt. I am also working on my own family tree, although more slowly as the records in countries where my ancestors are from, are either in a language I don't speak (Finland) or hard to access (Ukraine).

I have written up some of the more interesting stories I have uncovered during my research on my personal blog, including a story of a lawsuit for a "breach of promise" to marry a woman, a story about a very elderly bride and groom, and how my research into an old postcard took me across the Atlantic.
